Results

Expected project result are:
  • a report on the state of the play in autonomy in Kazakhstan
  • constructive discussions between universities and the Ministries on implementing decentralized university governance systems with higher degree of autonomy;
  • design of a basic model of university autonomy;
  • pilot implementation;
  • he creation of a Consulting Group with experts on implementation of autonomy models.
