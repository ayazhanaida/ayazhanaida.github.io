TRUNAK

TRANSITION TO UNIVERSITY AUTONOMY IN KAZAKHSTAN

project co-funded by the Erasmus+ Programme of the European Union

Project number
586205-ЕРР-1-2017-1-KZ-EPPKA2-CBHE-SP

Coordinated by
Karaganda State Medical University/KSMU

Project duration
15/10/2017 – 14/10/2020

The purpose of the project is to create a model of transition of Kazakhstani universities from the traditional form of management to autonomous management.
